About

Through achieving tremendous success in the manpower industry, Adiba Infotech Pvt. Ltd. is now extending its route to have a different unit in the market. By enabling the job seekers to find their dream jobs and placing the right candidate in the right place for the betterment of organizations, Adiba Infotech now endeavours to get prominence by working as a mediator between the builders and property buyers to give support to the vision of ‘Home for All’.

Understanding the importance of authorized real estate properties and how people struggle to get one, Adiba Infotech Pvt. Ltd. strives to lighten up their presence with an aim to revolutionize the process of how a real estate firm function. We came up with a group of passionate real estate experts, located in the city of joy - Kolkata, to provide utmost customer satisfaction. We are well equipped with experts managing different departments with a focus on evaluating a project in detail to provide the exact detail and flawless results to our customers. Our professionals are highly skilled and well trained to understand customer’s requirements and deliver the best result up to their expectations. With endless dedication, our team can solve every customer’s property searching complexities.

We at Adiba Infotech, not only believe in providing the best result to your house hunt, but also in educating you in the best possible way covering each parameter of your property. Therefore, we ensure you a smooth property searching experience like never before. Simultaneously, builders are getting an authorized source and easiest way to present their properties or any types of houses without any hassle.
